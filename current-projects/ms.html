MicroStrategies

Empowering Businesses with Cutting-Edge IT Solutions

Background & Context

MicroStrategies, founded in 1983 by Anthony Bongiovanni in New Jersey, started as a custom software provider and grew into a leader in IT solutions, offering services like cloud computing, business analytics, and security.

Q Marketing partnered with MicroStrategies to enhance its brand positioning in a complex IT landscape, helping clients navigate technology challenges. See our mission.

Challenges

  • Complexity of Modern IT: Navigating intricate tech stacks and cybersecurity threats.
  • Staying Competitive: Adapting to rapid technological changes.
  • Client Decision-Making: Guiding clients to align tech with business goals.

Our Strategy

  • Consultative Approach: Deep collaboration to understand client needs.
  • Leveraging Partnerships: Using IBM and Microsoft ties for tailored solutions.
  • Focus on Business Outcomes: Designing IT solutions for measurable success.

Implementation

  • Technology Solution Centers: Offering demos and proof-of-concept testing.
  • Expert Architecture & Design: Seamless integration into client systems.
  • Client-Centric Execution: Custom implementations for specific goals.

Results & Outcomes

  • Served Hundreds of Clients: Improved efficiency and security across industries.
  • Expanded Capabilities: 2016 acquisition of New England Systems boosted analytics.
  • Industry Recognition: Won iManage 2023 Revenue Growth Partner award.

Key Takeaways

MicroStrategies’ success highlights the value of adaptability and client focus in IT. Our partnership reinforced their leadership through innovative branding. Contact us to elevate your IT strategy.
