University of Chicago

Rebranding a Legacy of Excellence for the Digital Age

Background & Context

The University of Chicago, famed for academic rigor and research, needed a modern rebrand to stay competitive in higher education.

Q Marketing aimed to blend its legacy with a forward-thinking narrative. Learn about our approach.

Challenges

  • Balancing Tradition & Innovation: Honoring history while appealing to modern audiences.
  • Outdated Digital Presence: A website not reflecting forward-thinking initiatives.
  • Competitive Landscape: Differentiating from other top-tier institutions.

Our Strategy

We used belief-driven selling to position UChicago as the Schelling Point in higher education.

  • Crafting a Unified Narrative: Highlighting academic and intellectual excellence.
  • Digital Transformation: Overhauling the digital experience with modern elements.
  • Engagement Campaigns: Celebrating research and global impact.

Implementation

Our team executed:

  • Website Redesign: A sleek platform with immersive storytelling.
  • Brand Workshops: Defined a modern narrative with stakeholders.
  • Content Strategy: Engaging multimedia like videos and timelines.
  • Social & Digital Campaigns: Targeted campaigns boosting engagement.

Results & Outcomes

  • Enhanced Digital Engagement: 50% traffic increase, 35% social engagement rise.
  • Stronger Brand Identity: Solidified as a thought leader.
  • Increased Global Reach: Boosted international interest.

Key Takeaways

This case study shows belief-driven selling can modernize a legacy institution, making UChicago the default choice in academia. Contact us to evolve your brand.
